# Ticket 2: Enable calendar integration

**Description**  
The "Enable calendar integration" feature allows users to connect their personal calendars with the Coworking App. This integration enables users to manage their bookings and events directly within the app, helping them keep track of their coworking schedules and upcoming networking opportunities. By incorporating calendar integration, the app enhances user organization and ensures that users do not miss important bookings or events, thereby improving their overall experience in managing their workspaces and professional connections.

**Functionality**  
- Users can link their external calendars (e.g., Google Calendar, Outlook) to the Coworking App.
- Users can view their coworking bookings and events alongside their personal calendar events.
- Users receive notifications for upcoming bookings and events directly from their integrated calendar.
- Users can create new events or bookings in the app that automatically sync with their external calendar.
- Users can edit or delete bookings in the app, with changes reflected in their external calendar.
- Users can set reminders for upcoming coworking events or bookings based on their calendar settings.
- Users can filter calendar views to display only coworking-related events or bookings.

**Requirements**  
- The feature must support integration with popular calendar services such as Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook.
- The app must provide a user interface for users to authenticate and link their external calendar accounts securely.
- The app must retrieve and display events from the linked calendars in a user-friendly format.
- The app must allow users to create, edit, and delete events or bookings that sync with their external calendars.
- The app must implement a notification system to alert users of upcoming bookings and events based on their calendar settings.
- The feature must include options for users to customize reminder settings for events and bookings.
- The implementation must ensure that user data is handled securely and complies with data privacy regulations.
